OUR STORY

Built for the Complexity of the Gulf

StratEdge Advisory was founded on a conviction: that businesses operating in Oman and the wider GCC operate in one of the world's most sophisticated — and most demanding — commercial environments. The regulatory depth, the relational dynamics, the pace of Vision 2040, and the structural complexities of Gulf markets require a different quality of advisory counsel than most firms are equipped to provide.

We are not a generalist consultancy. We do not operate from templates or offshore delivery centres. We are a compact, senior-led advisory practice that engages directly with leadership — because the decisions we are asked to support are too consequential for anything less.

Every engagement begins with the same commitment: to understand your situation completely, advise you honestly, and deliver outcomes that hold under scrutiny — legally, commercially, and strategically.
