Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

At StratEdge Advisory, we recognize that the confidentiality of your information is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard any personal or corporate data shared with us through our digital presence.

1. Information We Collect

When you engage with us through our digital consultation request form, we collect the following limited information:

  • Full Name and Professional Title
  • Company or Organization Name
  • Professional Email Address and Contact Number
  • Inquiry Details regarding our Advisory Pillars

2. Purpose of Processing

Any data collected is used solely for the purpose of initiating a preliminary advisory consultation and verifying the commercial context of your inquiry. We do not use your data for automated marketing or share it with third-party data brokers.

3. Strategic Confidentiality

As a boutique advisory firm, we maintain high standards of professional secrecy. Information shared via our website is treated as "Pre-Engagement Data" and is protected by internal security protocols until a formal Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is executed during our first consultation.

4. Data Retention

Inquiries that do not lead to a formal engagement are purged from our digital logs within 180 days, unless a legal requirement for retention exists under Omani commercial law.

5. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy and all matters related to the handling of data by StratEdge Advisory are governed by the laws of the **Sultanate of Oman** and relevant international data protection standards applied to professional services.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding our data handling or confidentiality framework, please contact us directly through the consultation form on our home page.
